About Us

Welcome to our website! Here's a little bit about who we are and what we do.

Mission

To empower individuals through education, innovation, and community engagement.

Vision

To create a world where every individual has access to quality education and opportunities for growth and development.

Our Story

Founded in 1850, V.M. GOVT HSS PERIYAKULAM is managed by the Department of Education and is located in Urban area. We have been dedicated to providing quality education and fostering a nurturing environment for our students. From our humble beginnings, we have grown into a dynamic force for positive change in our community.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in everything we do, setting high standards for ourselves and our community.
  • Inclusivity: We embrace diversity and foster an inclusive environment where everyone feels valued and respected.
  • Innovation: We encourage creativity, curiosity, and innovation as catalysts for positive change.
